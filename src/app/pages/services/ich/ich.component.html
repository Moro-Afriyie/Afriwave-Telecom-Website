Interconnect ClearingHouse (ICH)

the Interconnect ClearingHouse

In 2015, Afriwave Telecom Ghana Limited secured the license from the Government of Ghana through the National Communications Authority to build and operate facilities for the provision and operation of ClearingHouse services, pursuant to the provisions of National Communications Act, 2008 (Act,769)

Benefit of ICH

The ICH has among others:

  • Sanitized the telecom industry by providing the platform to reduce billing disputes amongst Service Providers, and thus minimizing cases requiring mediation by the Regulator.
  • Reduced CapEx and OpEx in setting up and maintaining interconnect links to other telecom and service providers.
  • Enhanced interconnect cashflow and improve liquidity within the interconnect regime.
  • Provided peculiar non-parallel infrastructure for the routing of International voice/SMS traffic by Licensed International Wholesale Carriers (IWCs).
  • Created a single point for all Value-Added Service (VAS) providers to gain access to the Mobile Network Operators (MNOs) from one source.

The Interconnect Clearing house (ICH) has three (3) points of Interconnection in three different geographical locations which carries 100% National Interconnect Traffic and International Traffic from International Wholesale Carriers to the Mobile Network Operators.

  • All our data centers have been strategically positioned for easy access to existing service provider networks
  • Have been dimensioned and configured for automatic traffic recovery and load sharing.
  • Connectivity between data centers is enhanced by complete fibre optics cable network.

These provide clients with efficient and cost-effective options for interconnect.
