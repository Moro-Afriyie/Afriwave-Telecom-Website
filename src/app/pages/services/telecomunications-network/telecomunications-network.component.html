SMS Services

As part of the license awarded to Afriwave Telecom by the National Communications Authority, Afriwave is also to connect and route SMS traffic among service providers and to monitor traffic volumes of each service provider.

To drive effective SMS communication between businesses and clients, Afriwave Telecom have built an SMS platform called “APPLICATION-to-PERSON” (A2P) for all Valued Added Services (VAS) and Content providers to get to the MNOs from one place through the ICH.

The following are the benefits of the SMS platform:

  • The ICH “SMS” platform creates a single point of access to VAS & Content Providers and other components of the economy.
  • Low entry barrier for VAS and content providers to get to the MNOs.
  • Due to reduced entry barrier and the cost-effective as well as the simplified interconnect architecture, Content and Value-Added Service Providers as well as other Sectors of the Economy will be attracted to the telecom ecosystem.
  • Through the ICH “SMS” platform customers shall be opened to more Content Services and other Sectors of the Economy.
