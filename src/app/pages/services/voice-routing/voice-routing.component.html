Voice Traffic Routing

Afriwave Telecom is at the forefront of innovative Voice Traffic Routing Services in Ghana. Operating as a carrier neutral voice traffic routing entity, Afriwave plays a pivotal role in routing National Interconnect Traffic (Off-net traffic) and International Interconnect Traffic (Inbound and Outbound) through International Wholesale Carrier Licensees (IWCLs). Our established network ensures reliable communication for Mobile Network Operators (MNOs) and International Wholesale Carriers (IWCs), as well as any NCA licensed Connecting Entities (CEs), offering essential benefits to the telecom landscape.

Key Benefits of Afriwave's Routing National & International Interconnect Voice Traffic:

  • Reconciliation of Interconnect Service Traffic: Afriwave's robust infrastructure ensures seamless reconciliation of Interconnect Service Traffic. Through our meticulous processes, we enhance the accuracy and reliability of interconnect traffic records.
  • Comprehensive Traffic Settlement Reports: We provide comprehensive interconnect service traffic settlement reports, facilitating regulatory compliance. These reports contribute to transparency and accountability within the telecom industry.
  • Strategic Geographical Data Centre Redundancy: Afriwave's strategic positioning of its three data centers brings enhanced network redundancy. With any one data center capable of handling 100% of total off-net voice traffic, we ensure uninterrupted communication services.
  • Efficient Traffic Recovery and Load-Sharing:Our ICH setup incorporates an advanced routing algorithm that automates traffic recovery and load-sharing. This mechanism ensures optimized traffic distribution, bolstering the efficiency of the entire system.

Afriwave Telecom is committed to ensuring the seamless flow of voice traffic, contributing to a well-functioning interconnect ecosystem. As a vital link between MNOs and IWCs, we prioritize reliability, transparency, and efficiency in every aspect of our operations.
