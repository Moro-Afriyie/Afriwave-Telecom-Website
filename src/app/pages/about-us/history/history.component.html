Our History

Established in 1998 to provide services to meet the rising demand of telecommunications as the industry began to boom.

It was the dream made a reality by a group of dynamic people who saw the strategic need of such a service provider as the dawn of telecommunications and digital age beckoned.

In February 2015, Afriwave received authorization as Ghana's first Interconnect Clearing House license after a competitive bidding process against four other applicants.

As a licensee, Afriwave has the mandate to provide a common, independent mechanism for monitoring, routing, billing and settlement of local and international interconnect traffic for all existing and future telecommunications operators in the country.

Afriwave Telecom is in the league of local leaders providing telecom solutions and has a track record of utilizing competent skills and expertise to provide a complete range of professional and end-to-end telecommunication services in the country.

Interconnect Clearing House (ICH) License Scope & Deliverables

Per the amended license issued to Afriwave by the National Communications Authority, Afriwave is licensed to provide the following:

  • The Licensee shall provide capacity and routing for both IP and TDM based traffic and to ensure full interoperability of all networks and service providers in the country.
  • Nothing in this Licence takes away the need to obtain any other Licence for the purposes of providing such other services that are not covered by this Licence and as may be required under the Act, EC Act, EC Amendment Act or any other enactment.
  • The Licensee shall obtain all necessary licences and permits including building permits and other non-telecommunication permits required to build, implement, modify and remove installations and building in accordance with the applicable laws and regulations in Ghana.
  • Provision of data packet measurement and differentiation tools for Quality of Service and Price Regulatory compliance.
  • Connectivity to Number Portability Services, Bank Switch, and Internet Connect Exchange respectively as and when required.
  • The capability, if needed, to host local and international Over-The-Top (OTT) service providers.
  • The capability to provide a common infrastructure for Government Agencies to host ICT Systems and Applications and to store confidential data securely.
  • This Licence shall be for the provision and operation of Interconnect Clearinghouse services using radio, cable, satellite or a combination of any of these systems deployed for the purpose of providing point-to-point or point-to-multipoint communication for the connection to service providers for the conveyance of voice, data and video.
  • The Licensee shall maintain the capability to connect and route voice, SMS, MMS, or any other telecommunications traffic among service provider and to monitor traffic volumes of each service provider.
  • The Licensee shall provide prevent fraud on its system.
  • The Licensee shall provide the relevant information for the monthly reconciliation between Connecting Entities.

The Licensee (Afriwave Telecom) is authorised to connect to Interconnect Clearinghouse to:

  • Any other telecommunications network operated under a Licence granted by the Authority in accordance with the Act.
  • Any recognized telecommunications network outside Ghana.
  • Any earth orbiting apparatus in accordance with applicable requirement.
  • Any terminal equipment approved by the Authority.
